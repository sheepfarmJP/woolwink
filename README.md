# Woolwink

**See it. Ask it.** One hotkey puts your screenshot — and your voice, typed out — straight into your own AI chat (Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini). 100% on your PC.

> Windows 10/11 · Works with the Claude / ChatGPT / Gemini desktop apps **and** browser tabs · 8 languages

---

## What it does

Press a hotkey → Woolwink grabs your screen (or a region, a short recording, a long scrolling page, or the **last 10 minutes** via a rewind buffer), optionally turns your **voice into text** locally, lets you review and mark it up, then drops it **directly into your AI's input box** using your own account. No more screenshot → find file → drag → drop → type.

## Why it's different

- 🔒 **100% local processing.** Capture, OCR and voice-to-text all run on your PC. We have no server and never receive your data. The only network use is an optional update check and a one-time AI-model download — neither sends your screen, voice, or text.
- 🧩 **Neutral, multi-AI.** Insert into **Claude, ChatGPT or Gemini** — desktop app or browser tab. It's *your* account; Woolwink just delivers the content.
- ⏪ **10-minute rewind.** A RAM-only buffer lets you grab something that already happened.
- 🎙 **Local voice → text** (Whisper), 📜 **scrolling capture**, 🔤 **OCR + auto-blur** of emails / keys / card numbers before anything is sent.
