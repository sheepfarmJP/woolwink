FAQ

Does my data go to your servers?

No. Woolwink has no servers and no account. Everything runs on your PC. When you send a capture, it goes into your own AI app under your own subscription — exactly as if you pasted it yourself.

How does it put things into the AI?

It brings the AI's window or browser tab to the front and pastes your capture into the input box — like a very fast copy-paste. The target app/tab must be open and visible on screen. For browser targets it inserts only; you press Enter yourself.

Which AIs work?

Claude, ChatGPT and Gemini — both the desktop apps and the browser tabs — plus a custom target. You use your own logged-in account.

Is OCR accurate in my language?

OCR uses Windows' built-in engine, so it's accurate for the languages whose Windows OCR pack is installed (usually your system language). The dashboard shows which are installed.
